# Styling

- Use CSS variables.
- Build dark-first UI.
- Subtle glass effects are allowed.
- No Tailwind class-string sprawl.
- Cards may be used for repeated items, modals, and framed tools.
- Avoid giant design systems before repeated needs exist.
- Text must not overflow controls or overlap other content at mobile and desktop widths.
- Do not rely on color alone to communicate status.
